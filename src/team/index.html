Change is a group effort

Covi Education was incorporated as a 501(c)3 nonprofit in 2015. Since then we have worked with researchers and professors from over 25 universities and trained educators from 100+ schools nationwide. Our projects are heavily supported by committed volunteers - not all of whom can be listed here - who generously donate their time to collaboratively change the way science, society, and the individual interact.

If you’re interesting in partnering, supporting a project or joining the team, please email hello@thepeoplesscience.org.
